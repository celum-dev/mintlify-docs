VirtCloud gives you a powerful, flexible platform for running virtual machines in the cloud. Whether you need a single development VM or a fleet of production servers connected by private networks, VirtCloud handles the entire lifecycle — from provisioning to backups — through a clean dashboard and a fully programmable REST API.

## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.virtcloud.io](https://app.virtcloud.io) and verify your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provision your first VM">
    Follow the [Quick Start guide](/quickstart) to launch your first virtual machine from the dashboard or via the API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect and build">
    SSH into your VM, attach storage, configure networking, and start building your infrastructure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Looking for the API? Head to the [API Reference](/api/overview) to explore all available endpoints and start automating your infrastructure.
</Note>
